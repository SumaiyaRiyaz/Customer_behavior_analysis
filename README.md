# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to uncover insights related to purchasing patterns, customer segmentation, product performance, and revenue trends.

The analysis is performed using **SQL, Python, and Power BI**, demonstrating how data can be transformed into meaningful business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchase behavior
* Identify high-performing products
* Understand the impact of discounts and subscriptions
* Segment customers based on purchase history
* Compare revenue across different customer groups

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** – Data querying and analysis
* **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)** – Data cleaning & visualization
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard creation
* **Excel/CSV Dataset**

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Dataset used: `customer_shopping_behavior.csv`
* Contains information such as:

  * Customer ID
  * Gender
  * Age Group
  * Purchase Amount
  * Product Category
  * Review Ratings
  * Subscription Status
  * Shipping Type
  * Discount Applied

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis (SQL)

### 1. Revenue by Gender

* Compared total revenue generated by male vs female customers

### 2. High-Spending Discount Users

* Identified customers who used discounts but still spent above average

### 3. Top Rated Products

* Found top 5 products based on average review ratings

### 4. Shipping Comparison

* Compared average purchase amount between **Standard vs Express shipping**

### 5. Subscription Impact

* Analyzed:

  * Average spending
  * Total revenue
  * Customer count
    between subscribers and non-subscribers

### 6. Discount Trends

* Identified products with the highest discount usage percentage

### 7. Customer Segmentation

Customers classified into:

* **New** (1 purchase)
* **Returning** (2–10 purchases)
* **Loyal** (>10 purchases)

### 8. Top Products per Category

* Extracted top 3 most purchased products in each category

### 9. Repeat Buyers vs Subscription

* Checked if repeat buyers are more likely to subscribe

### 10. Revenue by Age Group

* Analyzed contribution of different age groups to total revenue

## 🚀 Key Insights

* Subscription status impacts overall revenue significantly
* Loyal customers contribute a major portion of sales
* Certain products perform well even with discounts
* Age group and gender influence purchasing behavior
* Express shipping users may show different spending patterns
